1
Perplexity sweep (editorial coverage)
Open Perplexity, set Focus to News, paste the query below and filter to last 24 hours. Do not clean or filter results before bringing them back here.
"Bob Zangrillo" OR "Magic City Innovation District" OR "Magic City Miami"
Filter: last 24 hours
Output: publication name, headline, date, author if available, URL
Group by tier: national wire first, then regional, then trade, then blogs, then social
Exclude: event listings, casino results, unrelated Magic City references
2
Google Video sweep (social and creator content)
Perplexity misses video and social content almost entirely. Run this separately on Google.
Go to google.com and search:
"Bob Zangrillo" OR "Magic City Innovation District"

Click the Videos tab. Scroll through two pages. Screenshot results and bring back here.
You are looking for: TikTok, Instagram reels, YouTube, LinkedIn video, Facebook video.
Any platform not captured in the Perplexity sweep.
3
Paste everything to Claude
Paste the Perplexity output and drop the Google screenshots into the tracking conversation. Claude cleans it, deduplicates, tiers outlets, scores sentiment, adds new rows to the coverage log, and updates the assessment if the trajectory has shifted. No prep needed on your end.
